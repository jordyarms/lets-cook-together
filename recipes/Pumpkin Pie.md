## Ingredients

- [ ] [M.::1 cup]   [[Brown Sugar]]
- [ ] [M.::1/2 tsp]  [[Salt]]
- [ ] [M.::1 tsp]  [[Cinnamon]]
- [ ] [M.::2]  [[Eggs]]
- [ ] [M.::420 ml]  [[Pumpkin, Canned]]
- [ ] [M.::1 cup] of  [[Cream]]

## Directions
- mix ingredients together
- add to pie shell
- bake 450°C for 20 mins
- reduce to 350°C for 25 min or firm
